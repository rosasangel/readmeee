Tennis

Tennis is my favorite sport to play because of how competitive and exciting it is. You don't just hit a ball back and fourth, you have to be strategic and able to react quickly. My friends and I like to get together a few times a month and play a couple matches. The time goes by so fast that we don't realize that we've played the whole day.

Carpentry

I love doing carpentry work because I get a feeling of accomplishment every time I finish working on something. Knowing that I'm able to create or repair things with my hands helps me recognize how fortunate I am. Also, by knowing how to work on door frames, install windows, build a shed, and etc, I'm able to save a lot of money by doing the work myself.

Painting

I started painting about one year ago. I began by using only acrylic paints and I would only paint smaller objects like trees and birds. After sometime I moved on to using oil paints and trying to do portraits of people and landscapes. This hobby also helps me relax, it's a very peaceful way to spend your time.
